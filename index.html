Lindsey & Ryan


When people ask us how we met the answer is very simple: volleyball. We can owe pretty much our whole relationship to volleyball and an obsessive love of The Office. After our first date on National Tequila Day we bonded over our absurd ability to quote The Office, consume Mexican Martinis, and win lots of games of doubles in sand volleyball.


From there we’ve discovered that we truly complement each other in all the best ways - from crushing friends in cornhole, to preferring breakfast tacos to basically any other food, and attending every country music concert we can go to. Now three and a half years - and two dogs - later, we can’t wait to celebrate, dance, and drink the night away with you!

The Wedding

Ceremony will begin at 6:00pm

Vintage Villas

Our celebration will take place at Vintage Villas on beautiful Lake Travis. The ceremony is at The Gazebo and the reception will follow just a few steps away!

There is plenty of parking available on site. Transportation will be available from The Westin at The Domain - more details to come!

Ride shares are also a great option, as per the Father of the Bride’s request, the open bar will be available for as long as legally possible. If you choose to use Ride Austin don’t forget to round up to Austin Pets Alive!
